about us.

Our team is motivated by a passion for people, technology and the art of storytelling. Technology and storytelling are always interlinked and, with the emergence of smartphones, we saw the potential to enhance and expand storytelling in innovative ways. The problem though is that people haven’t taken advantage of this new platform. At the moment, stories just happen to be on our phones; they’re not designed for and driven by their technology and capabilities.

For example, eBooks are digitized physical books tossed under a screen; films are the same on whatever device you view; audiobooks are the same.

But, what if a story was created first with the phone and its technology in mind?

We couldn’t find anything like that out there; so, we set out to build it. We wanted to explore and establish the rules and principles of mobile stories. oolipo is an experiment in creating new kind of stories that are designed specifically for our phones; stories that are enhanced by all the great technology we use every day.

Our vision is to develop a new platform and marketplace for serialized, media-driven storytelling. We exist to provide storytellers a space to showcase their creativity and imagination, and to connect with people everywhere who love great stories.

We’re excited to explore with you how technology can enhance the language, community and experience of stories in the digital space.
